Pizza

Description

This recipe will have you create a delicious pizza with pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, and green peppers as toppings.

Ingredients

The ingredients you need to create this pizza recipe are as follows:

  • pizza dough
  • sauce
  • cheese
  • pepperoni
  • mushrooms
  • olives
  • green peppers

Steps

The steps to create this recipe are as follows:

  1. make the pizza dough
  2. add sauce
  3. add toppings
  4. add cheese on top
  5. put it in the oven
  6. enjoy your pizza
