Turkey Burgers

Description

These turkey burgers are delicious and super easy/quick to make. They are perfect for a weeknight meal, and you can make enough for a few days of leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1.3lbs ground turkey
  • olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 shallot
  • parsley
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • dash of liquid smoke

Steps

  1. Mince/chop the garlic cloves, shallot, and parsley
  2. Add to the ground turkey alongside some olive oil, salt, pepper, worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke
  3. Combine all ingredients thoroughly and make 4 evenly-sized patties
  4. Heat up a cast iron skillet on med high until hot and add a bit of olive oil to it
  5. Add the patties to the skillet and cook on each side for about 5 minutes
  6. Serve on a roll with lettuce, tomato, and whatever other toppings and condiments
