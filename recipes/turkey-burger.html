Turkey Burger

Description

This recipe will have you create delicious turkey burger patties.

Ingredients

The ingredients you need to create this turkey burger recipe are as follows:

  • ground turkey
  • egg
  • garlic
  • shallots
  • parsley
  • worcestershire sauce
  • liquid smoke

Steps

The steps to create this recipe are as follows:

  1. chop the garlic, parsley, and shallots
  2. beat 1 egg until it looks consistent
  3. add the egg, garlic, parsley, shallots, worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke to the ground turkey
  4. create 4 patties
