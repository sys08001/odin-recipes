Chicken Piccata

Description

This recipe will have you create some delicious chicken piccata.

Ingredients

The ingredients you need to create this recipe are as follows:

  • chicken breasts
  • flour
  • parmesan cheese
  • oil
  • butter
  • chicken broth
  • lemon juice
  • white wine
  • garlic
  • capers
  • parsley

Steps

The steps to create this recipe are as follows:

  1. pound chicken breasts until they are 1/4 inch thick
  2. dredge in flour and parmesan cheese
  3. heat on medium in olive oil and butter and sear the chicken on both sides
  4. remove chicken from pan and set aside
  5. add white wine and garlic, reduce until very little liquid left
  6. add capers, chicken broth, lemon juice
  7. add chicken back to pan, spoon sauce on top and cover for 5 minutes
  8. reduce heat to low and swirl 2 tablespoons of cold butter
  9. tilt pan to distribute sauce and serve over the chicken
